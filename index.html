we like a challenge

Self-adhesive labels, shrink sleeves, flexible packaging and IML. We do make them in all shapes, colours and sizes - and, yes, we do like a challenge. So whether conventional or never-been-done-before, go ahead, ask us.

different goals, different labels

For every business our people know what makes a fine label tick: pharmaceutical, cosmetics, food & beverages, industrial & automotive, chemical or household. More than 50 years of dedication to label printing makes the difference.

but do you like a challenge?

We’ll probably start by asking questions. Because we will come up with a label solution that suits you better than the one at hand. That’s the Reynders way.
